RSS, and how to use it.
Published on:03/09/2020. Edited on 03/09/2020

RSS,or Really Simple Syndication, is a way to basically subscribe to websites. You'd use an RSS reader to view the latest posts and updates to a website. Most people use it to view the latest news from a dozen news sites without having to install a specific mobile app or going to each website individually.

Recommended RSS readers

I personally use Feeder on android to see the news every now and then. and while I haven't used it yet, I heard that Newsboat is a good RSS reader for MacOS and Gnu/Linux operating systems.

How to use RSS.

You just need to find this symbol on whatever website you're on and then copy the link of the RSS feed, then add it to your RSS reader. Now you should get the new posts as soon as they come out.
If the website dosen't have an RSS feed, then you can use fetchrss to generate an RSS feed to add to your RSS reader.

What about making your own RSS feed for your website?

I've tried to add an RSS feed to this website recently, and it's as easy as basic HTML. I recommend you check out this video by Tinkernut, since he can explain the process far better than I can do.
