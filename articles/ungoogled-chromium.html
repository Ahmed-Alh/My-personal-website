With Firefox becoming worse with every new update, one may ask the question "What is the best freedom and privacy respscting browser?"

What is Ungoogled Chromium?

Ungoogled Chromium is just that, Chromium, the open source web browser developed by Google which chrome is built on, but with Google tracking and telemetry completely removed. Chromium already had less Google integration compared to Chrome, and with the Ungoogled Chromium project, they are trying to make a completely Google-free web browser that is actually usable in 2020.
